Decoded is one of the first authentic NFT-driven fashion brands for hoodies in Europe. We offer hoodies in multiple colors, that are sustainable and made to be timeless because of their straightforward design, however, each hoodie has a special interactive code that distinguishes it from the rest. It distinguishes our buyers from everyone else and as said by Steve Jobs: “Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower”. Collecting art has changed throughout history and we change the way you buy it. We believe that we are leaders in combining fashion and the act of collecting art in a new way plus we help people to be brave and unleash their epicness (not a real word huh?). We give our people: a sense of identity, self-expression, and the chance to build “their” art collection as building their legacy.

What do we do?

Decoded works with top NFT artists and with each order you will receive a part from their world. However, in order to preserve the authenticity and uniqueness of each piece, you will be only able to see what you got once you have your delivery.

Vision

In a world where we all strive to be unique, we believe that we can do that with only one authentic piece in our wardrobe.
