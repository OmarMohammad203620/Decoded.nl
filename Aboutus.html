Decoded is one of the first authentic NFT-driven fashion brands for hoodies in Europe. Each hoodie that we sell has a V-code that could be scanned which will prove the patent and uniqueness of the garment. In case the garment is lost, damaged or sold to somebody else the V-code could be scanned and "Decoded" will send a new piece to the first and only owner in order he/she to have a new authentic piece, "Decoded" is a brand that challenges the collaboration between art and luxury fashion in a new way making the art wearable and one of a kind.
